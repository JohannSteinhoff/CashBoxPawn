Frequently Asked Questions

How does a pawn loan work?

You bring in an item of value, we appraise it for free and offer you a cash loan on the spot. Your item is stored safely, and when you repay the loan you get it back. There's no credit check and it never affects your credit score.

What happens if I can't repay my loan?

Nothing bad — your item simply becomes property of the store. There are no collection calls, no debt, and no impact on your credit. You're always welcome back.

What items do you accept?

Jewelry, gold, watches, firearms, tools, electronics, computers, cameras, gaming consoles, musical instruments, and much more. Not sure? Bring it by or give us a call at (512) 558-7296.

Do you buy items outright?

Yes — if you'd rather sell than pawn, we pay top dollar on the spot for quality items.

Do I need an ID?

Yes, Texas law requires a valid government-issued photo ID for all pawn and sale transactions.
